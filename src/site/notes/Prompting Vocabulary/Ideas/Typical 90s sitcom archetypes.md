**1. The Cool Guy / Slacker**

- Baggy light-wash jeans, oversized flannel shirt (unbuttoned over a graphic tee), worn-out Converse or work boots, backwards baseball cap

**2. The Preppy / Popular Girl**

- High-waisted light-wash jeans or plaid miniskirt, cropped cardigan over a baby tee, scrunchie in hair, white Keds or platform sneakers

**3. The Dad / Authority Figure**

- Light-wash dad jeans (slightly too short), boxy sweater vest over a collared polo, white sneakers with visible white socks, maybe a baseball cap indoors

**4. The Mom / Slightly Frumpy**

- Floor-length floral or geometric dress, oversized blazer with shoulder pads, clogs or sensible flats, pearl clip-on earrings, side ponytail or scrunchie

**5. The Teenage Sister / Sass Master**

- Overalls (one strap undone) over a brightly colored long-sleeve shirt, choker necklace, butterfly clips in hair, platform sneakers

**6. The Nerd / Socially Awkward**

- Too-tight mom jeans, polo shirt buttoned all the way up (or tucked in aggressively), fanny pack, Velcro sneakers, center-parted hair with too much gel

**7. The Goth / Alternative Kid**

- Black jeans or ripped tights under plaid skirt, oversized black hoodie or distressed band tee, chain wallet, black combat boots, dark lipstick

**8. The Jock / Athletic**

- Windbreaker pants in teal or purple, oversized team jersey (Bulls, Hornets, Raiders), fanny pack worn cross-body, chunky white sneakers (Reebok Pumps or similar), starter jacket tied around waist

---

These cover the standard 90s sitcom ensemble archetypes. Pick 4 that fit your scene characters.
