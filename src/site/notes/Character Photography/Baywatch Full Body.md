## Prompt

Ultra-realistic 8K wide-angle portrait of a female in her mid-20s. She has long voluminous blonde hair with caramel highlights styled in a sky-high ponytail with soft. Her grey-olive eyes catch the golden light vividly. Elegant facial structure, slim body, plump chest, and refined feminine proportions.

Captured during golden hour at Will Rogers State Beach in Pacific Palisades, Los Angeles. The scene shows her standing barefoot beside the iconic Baywatch lifeguard tower, scanning the coastline as a lifeguard. She holds a bright red Baywatch rescue float in one hand, the Pacific horizon glowing with sunset warmth behind her.

Mood and Concept:

Classic Baywatch cinematic energy with California glamour. Confident, radiant, and heroic tone evoking the 90s original series. The low-angle perspective enhances stature and presence, framed by the lifeguard tower and shimmering surf.

Outfit and Styling:

Authentic Baywatch one-piece lifeguard swimsuit in vivid red with round yellow-and-blue Baywatch Lifeguard emblem on the left hip area, matching the original design from the tv series. The cut features a scoop neckline and high-cut leg line for movement and realism. Barefoot on sand for authenticity. No jewelry except a waterproof digital wristwatch on her right wrist. Skin lightly tanned with a healthy, natural glow.

Make-up:

Golden-hour lifeguard glam. Dewy foundation with soft reflective warmth. Light bronze contour along cheekbones, coral blush blending into sunlit tones. Bronze shimmer eyeshadow with brown liner to intensify green eyes. Defined but natural lashes. Pink lips with soft gloss for vitality. Nails in barely-there neutral tint.

Hair:

Sky-high ponytail tied neatly with subtle elastic. Soft waves cascading down back with the wind lifting a few strands for natural motion.

Catchlight:

Warm amber catchlights from the setting sun glint in her grey-olive eyes, adding depth and cinematic realism.

Photography Specs:

Camera: Sony A7R V with 24 mm f/1.4 G Master lens

Aperture: f/1.8 for subject prominence and wide scene inclusion

Shutter speed: 1/500 s to capture crisp detail in motion

ISO: 100 for bright, clean coastal tones

Low-angle composition emphasizing sky, tower, and lifeguard posture

Aspect Ratio 5:7

Detail and Finish:

Cinematic color grading with controlled highlights, rich reds, and luminous skin. Sun flare halos along the lifeguard float and hair edges. Fine sand texture visible underfoot. Subtle contrast balance for print-ready realism. The final image evokes the spirit of Baywatch at golden hour. Confident, vibrant, and unmistakably Californian.
