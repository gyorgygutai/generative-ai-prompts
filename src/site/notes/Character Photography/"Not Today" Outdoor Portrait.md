## Prompt

Create a realistic low-quality amateur photograph taken by a beginner with a regular smartphone in casual snapshot style. The image must be highly consistent and reproducible every single time: exact same girl, exact same pose, exact same clothing, exact same camera angle and distance, exact same lighting, exact same location and exact same background elements. No variations allowed.

The photo is imperfect in a controlled way: slight consistent left tilt (5-7 degrees), soft focus in the center with slightly blurry edges, minor motion blur from handheld shake, bad but repeatable composition with awkward cropping on the right side, visible digital noise and heavy grain, poor white balance with slight warm yellowish cast, natural smartphone lens distortion.

Subject:
A stunningly beautiful 22-year-old woman with a fit, athletic look. She has fair skin with a light natural glow, bright hazel eyes, full natural lips, high cheekbones and a playful confident expression. She has long wavy dark brown hair. She has a perfectly proportional curvy figure with very big, visibly pumped and muscular thighs from years of heavy squats, thick juicy legs, narrow waist and toned midriff. She is wearing a very short tight denim mini skirt, a black cropped tank top with bold white English text "NOT TODAY" printed across the chest, and a fashionable old vintage trucker-style cap (faded dark blue with mesh back and worn embroidery, slightly tilted on her head).

Action and Pose (strictly locked):
She sits casually on the edge of an old wooden bridge, legs dangling over the side, body turned exactly in three-quarter view toward the camera. Both hands rest on the wooden railing beside her, one leg slightly bent. She looks directly at the camera with a relaxed, slightly mischievous smile. Exact same sitting pose every time.

Camera and Composition (strictly locked):
Full-body portrait shot from a low casual angle — camera positioned at hip/waist level (about 1.2–1.5 meters away), slightly from the front-left side. This perspective clearly shows her big muscular thighs, short skirt and the reptiles visible in the water below. Framing is always the same: she dominates the center, the wooden bridge planks and water take up the bottom third.

Lighting (strictly locked):
Bright midday sunlight in April, strong direct spring daylight from above and slightly from the right, creating realistic contrasts and soft highlights on her skin and thighs. Gentle natural shadows under the bridge.

Location/context (strictly locked):
Old wooden bridge somewhere in rural Louisiana, early April. Warm spring day. Under the bridge in the murky water a couple of American alligators and a couple of sharp-nosed American crocodiles are clearly visible swimming and sunbathing on the muddy bank. Background: green spring trees, calm swampy river, distant Louisiana bayou landscape. Exact same location and reptiles every time.

Style:
Unpolished casual smartphone snapshot style, documentary and lived-in, as if a friend quickly took the photo on a spring day. Everything must look real, slightly imperfect and spontaneous, but the core elements (exact same girl, exact sitting pose on the bridge, exact "NOT TODAY" top, exact trucker cap, exact low camera angle, exact bright April lighting, exact old wooden bridge in Louisiana with American alligators and sharp-nosed crocodiles below) remain 100% identical and reproducible every single generation. Photorealistic, high detail on skin texture, fabric wrinkles, wooden planks and reptiles.
