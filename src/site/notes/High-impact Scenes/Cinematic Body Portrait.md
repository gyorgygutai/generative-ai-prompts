## Prompt

Photorealistic cinematic film still, live-action (no CGI).

Three-quarter body portrait, vertical: Torso angled sideways, head toward camera. Hands gripping a rugged improvised shotgun at waist level. Wasteland survivor – Mad Max: Fury Road inspired. Practical, battle-worn realism. Environment: open desert wasteland, orange sand and dust clouds, saturated turquoise sky. Background: slightly blurred post-apocalyptic pursuit vehicle, sense of imminent chaos. Subtle motion blur and heat haze.

Lighting: harsh overhead desert sun, extremely hard shadows. Rich detail in sun-bleached physique, sense of dust-caked skin and deep shadow. Sweat and grease catching sharp specks of light.

Wardrobe & details: distressed leather, scarf around neck, dusty worn gear. Windblown messy hair. Face smeared with engine grease and sand (naturalistic).

Look & color: shot on 35mm film, Kodak Vision3 feel. Contrast. Strong teal & orange grading, realistic skin tones. Emphasis on tactile realism: rusted metal, sand particles, sweat, grease, fabric weave.

Final: raw, realistic; sun-blasted, high-energy cinematic still.
