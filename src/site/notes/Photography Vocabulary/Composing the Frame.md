Compositional techniques for arranging visual elements and controlling focus within the image.

- `rule of thirds` — dividing frame into ninths for balanced composition
- `shallow depth of field` — blurred background, focused subject
- `close-up portrait` — tight face framing for detail
- `medium shot` — mid-torso framing, conversational distance
- `full body shot` — entire figure visible in frame
- `dutch angle` — tilted frame, tension and dynamism
- `bird's eye view` — overhead perspective, comprehensive overview
- `worm's eye view` — ground-level perspective, magnification
- `negative space` — intentional empty area around subject for balance
