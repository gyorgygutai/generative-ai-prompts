Shot type and framing choices for how much of the subject is visible and distance from camera.

- `headshot` — face and shoulders only, intimate detail focus
- `three-quarters pose` — body turned slightly, adds depth
- `cowboy shot` — waist up, torso and face, conversational
- `upper body` — from shoulders down to hips
- `full body shot` — entire figure visible head to toe
- `close-up portrait` — extreme tight framing on face
