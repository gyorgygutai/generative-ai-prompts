Descriptors for character posture, stance, and seated/standing poses used in prompt composition.

## Standing & Seated Positions

- `standing` — neutral upright posture
- `sitting` — engaged seated position
- `kneeling` — character on knees
- `crouching` — compact low position
- `lying down` — horizontal reclined pose

## Dynamic Postures

- `contrapposto` — upper body twisted opposite to lower body (natural, dynamic)
- `weight shift` — one hip raised, natural stance
- `arching back` — spine bend with contrapposto effect
- `leaning against wall` — casual supported pose

## Arm & Hand Positioning

- `arms crossed` — defensive or casual posture
- `arms raised` — surrender, greeting, or celebration
- `hands on hips` — confident stance
- `hands behind back` — formal or restrained pose
- `hand on head` — contemplative or casual gesture
