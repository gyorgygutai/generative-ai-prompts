Focal length and aperture settings for depth of field, distortion, and optical effect.

## Focal Length

- `85mm lens` — portrait standard, flattering compression, minimal distortion
- `50mm lens` — natural perspective, no compression or wide effect
- `35mm lens` — slight wide angle, environmental context, subtle distortion
- `135mm lens` — heavy compression, strong background separation, portrait

## Aperture & Depth of Field

- `f/1.4` — extreme shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh
- `f/1.8` — pronounced shallow depth, smooth background blur
- `f/2.8` — moderate shallow depth, more scene context in focus
- `shallow depth of field` — blurred background, sharp subject focus
- `bokeh` — aesthetic background blur quality
- `lens blur` — smooth, rounded background blur

## Specialty Techniques

- `tilt-shift` — selective focus, miniature effect, horizontal band of sharpness
- `macro lens` — extreme close-up detail magnification, extreme depth compression
